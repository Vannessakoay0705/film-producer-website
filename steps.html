Step by Step Guide to Becoming a Film Producer

Home | Steps | Inspiration | My Journey | Favorite Films

As a Year 2 student of UniMAP in major New Media Communication, I am currently preparing for real-world experience through internships, while steadily working toward my graduation.

  1. Gain hands-on experience through internships during university studies.
  2. Successfully complete and graduate with a degree in New Media Communication
  3. Continue building professional connections within the film and media industry.
  4. Start working as a Production Assistant or in small production teams.
  5. Learn essential production skills: budgeting, scheduling, coordinating film sets.
  6. Create and produce independent short films to build a portfolio.
  7. Submit projects to film festivals and competitions to gain exposure.
  8. Gradually take on larger projects and leadership roles in film production.
